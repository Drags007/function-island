What is FUNC Token?

FUNC Token is the centrepiece to Function Island on TRON - a TRC20 Deflationary Staking token. FUNC aims to provide increasing value for any holders who stake the token, in a simple and intuitive suite of Dapps, with Open Source code and Verified Contracts.

How can I use FUNC?

The best value can be gained from FUNC in a number of ways. The most direct method is staking - you will earn more FUNC tokens, as other holders unstake or transfer their FUNC tokens around the network. There are other ways too, such as trading on JustSwap, or Poloniex.

What is a Username?

Your username is your personal tag, across all of Function Island - the single piece of identity that is tied to your wallet address - your greatest online handle. Make it a good one!

Registering an Username costs 10 TRX - a small fee to prevent namesquatting, which is collected by the Core Developer Team for maintenance of the platform.
